About Us

In October 2019, in an effort to continue to find ways to create more direct experience
with their community at large, Aslan opened its third location in the heart of the
Fremont neighborhood in Seattle. ASLAN BREWING SEATTLE is an outpost for all
of theirspecialty and one-off beers to which Seattleites simply do not have access.
